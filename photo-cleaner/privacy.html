Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Swipe Photo Cleaner ("Photo Cleanup", "the App") is an iOS application designed to help you quickly clean up your photo library using swipe gestures. Your privacy is important to us, and the App is built with a privacy-first approach.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not gather names, email addresses, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other data. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and no tracking of any kind.

Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your device's photo library solely to:

  • Display your photos so you can review them.
  • Delete photos you choose to remove.

All photo processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded, copied to external servers, or shared with any third party. The App uses Apple's Photos framework and respects the permissions you grant through iOS.

Data Storage

The App stores minimal data on your device only, such as your review progress and preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Subscriptions

The App may offer optional subscriptions for premium features. All subscriptions are processed and managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any billing details. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles payment data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the App collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
