ScanPro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Overview

ScanPro is developed by an independent developer. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects (or doesn't collect) and how it is handled.

Data Collection

ScanPro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All scanned documents, images, and PDFs are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

ScanPro does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Subscriptions

ScanPro offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions and a lifetime purchase managed through Apple's App Store. Payment is processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Camera & Photo Library

ScanPro requests access to your device's camera solely to scan documents. The app does not access your photo library. Camera data is processed on-device and is never transmitted externally.

Data Storage

All scanned documents are stored on your device using local storage. If you delete the app, all stored documents are permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

ScanPro does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ruima.dev@gmail.com.
